The Wedding Venue

The wedding and reception will take place at the La Mariposa Resort in Tucson Arizona.

Speedway & Houghton
1501 N Houghton Road
Tucson, AZ 8549

Using GPS navigation is not super reliable for this location as it has a couple entrances and most will be closed off. The best way to get to the venue:

From I-10
Exit I-10 at Exit 275 and head north for about 12 miles. Turn left onto Corte Vista la Mariposa and follow that road into the resort.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear?
Nothing too fancy, but nothing to shabby either. Somewhere inbetween dinner with your in-laws and meeting the queen.
How do I get to the venue?
From I-10: Take exit 275 and head north for about 12 miles. Turn left onto Corte Vista la Mariposa and follow that road into the resort.
What kind of shoes should I wear?
This will be an outdoor cerimony but an indoor reception. It might not be the best place for stilettos.

Will food be served?
Yes, we will be having an appetizer of chips, salsa, and guacamole; the entree is your choice of carne asada tacos or chile rellenos; and a dessert bar.
