The Wedding Venue

The wedding and reception will take place at the La Mariposa Resort in Tucson Arizona.

Speedway & Houghton
1501 N Houghton Road
Tucson, AZ 8549

Using GPS navigation is not super reliable for this location as it has a couple entrances and most will be closed off. The best way to get to the venue:

From I-10
Exit I-10 at Exit 275 and head north for about 12 miles. Turn left onto Corte Vista la Mariposa and follow that road into the resort.
